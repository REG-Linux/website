# WonderSwan

## Overview

Introduced in 1999 by Bandai, the WonderSwan was a portable system. Its platform tag is `wonderswan` for proper filtering.

## Technical specifications

- CPU: NEC V30MZ-compatible core clocked at 3.072 MHz.
- Memory: 64 KB RAM with 8 KB video RAM supporting grayscale graphics.
- Display: 224×144 reflective LCD in four shades of gray with flicker-free hardware sprite drawing.
- Sound: Mono audio engine with two square-wave generators and noise channel.
