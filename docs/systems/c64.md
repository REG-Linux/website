## Overview

Introduced in 1982 by Commodore, the Commodore 64 was a computer system. It is grouped with c64 titles in EmulationStation. Its platform tag is `c64` for proper filtering.

## Technical specifications

- CPU: MOS Technology 6510 running at 1.023 MHz (PAL) / 0.985 MHz (NTSC) with an integrated I/O port.
- Memory: 64 KB RAM, 20 KB ROM (BASIC, KERNAL, character ROM) plus ROM/RAM cartridges.
- Display: VIC-II chip with 320×200 resolution, 16 colors, 8 hardware sprites, and smooth scrolling.
- Sound: SID 6581/8580 chip delivering three oscillators, multi-mode filters, and advanced waveform synthesis.
