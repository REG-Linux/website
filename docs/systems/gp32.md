## Overview

Introduced in 2001 by GamePark, the GP32 was a portable system.

## Technical specifications

- CPU: ARM920T processor clocked at 133 MHz.
- Memory: 8 MB SDRAM plus 2 MB NOR flash for firmware and storage.
- Display: 320×240 transflective TFT LCD capable of 65,536 colors.
- Sound: AC97-compatible codec with stereo DAC for PCM playback.
