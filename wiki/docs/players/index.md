# Players

This section is for people who want a great play experience with minimal setup.

## Suggested path

- Pick your hardware: [Devices](../devices/index.md).
- Configure your controller: [Controller setup](controller-setup.md).
- Learn which systems are supported: [Emulated systems](../systems/index.md).
- Check content engines and native ports: [Engines](../engines/index.md) and [Ports](../ports/index.md).
- If you have not flashed yet, start at [Start here](../start-here/index.md).

## Not sure where you fit?

If you are helping maintain device support, jump to [Maintainers](../maintainers/index.md). If you are building images, jump to [Developers](../developers/index.md).
